Everything you need

📊
Smart savings counter
Feed Flow! measures exactly how much formula you prepare vs. consume. It shows you the savings in real time — in cans, in money, in something tangible.
⚡
One-handed logging
Designed for exhausted parents holding a baby. Log a feed in under two seconds, any time of day or night, without needing two hands or a clear head.
🔮
Predicts the next feed
After a week of logging, Feed Flow! learns your baby's rhythm and tells you when they'll likely be hungry next. Less guessing, more sleep.
👨‍👩‍👧
Share with caregivers
Partner, grandparent, nanny — invite up to 5 people to see and log feeds in real time. Free for everyone. No one is left guessing "when did they last eat?"
🥣
Tracks everything
Formula, breastfeeding, pumped milk, water, and solid foods as your baby grows. One app, the whole feeding journey.
📈
Analytics that matter
Daily totals, weekly trends, growth charts vs. WHO percentiles. The kind of data your pediatrician actually asks about — ready to share as a PDF report.
🔒

Your baby's data stays yours

Feed Flow! stores everything on your device and syncs privately via your own iCloud account. We never see your data, never sell it, and have no backend servers holding your family's information.
